The morning-line favourite and deserving one on form — won both the Sam F. Davis Stakes and the Arkansas Derby, blitzing the field by four lengths in the latter with a devastating turn of foot. Trained by Hall of Famer Todd Pletcher and ridden by five-time Eclipse Award winner Irad Ortiz Jr. But the post draw has thrown up a serious question mark.
⚠️ The concern: Renegade drew Post 1 — the rail. No horse has won the Kentucky Derby from Post 1 since 1986. That's 39 consecutive years without a winner from the inside gate. Pletcher's Derby record is 2 wins from 65 starters, and both victories came on sloppy tracks. Irad Ortiz Jr. is winless in nine Derby mounts. The form is there — but the stars are aligned against him.

The RacingEdge Call

Renegade is the class act on form but the rail draw is a serious handicap. No horse has won from Post 1 in 39 years. With Pletcher's poor Derby strike rate and Ortiz yet to win this race, the market may be overestimating him.

The Puma is our NAP. He lost the Florida Derby by a nose to Commandment — who goes off at 6/1. The Puma is available at 7/1. That's a genuine overlay. Javier Castellano won this race in 2023 with Mage from a similar position in the market. Post 9 gives him the outside run he needs. This horse has beaten or pushed every top rival in this field.

Further Ado is our each-way play — that Blue Grass demolition job was extraordinary and John Velazquez is a three-time winner of this race. Post 18 is the only concern.
